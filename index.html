our Services

Residential Roofing

Protect your home with our exceptional residential roofing services. Our team specializes in installing and repairing various roofing materials, including asphalt shingles, metal roofs, and tile roofs. We will work closely with you to find the perfect roofing solution that complements your home's style and provides long-lasting protection.

Commercial Roofing

Oraya Roofing understands the unique requirements of commercial properties. We have the expertise to handle commercial roofing projects of all sizes, from office buildings to retail spaces. Our commercial roofing services include installation, repair, and maintenance, ensuring that your business is well-protected and your operations run smoothly.

Roof Repair

Is your roof leaking, damaged, or showing signs of wear and tear? Our skilled technicians are here to provide prompt and efficient roof repair services. We will assess the extent of the damage and offer the most cost-effective solutions to restore the integrity and functionality of your roof.

Roof Replacement

If your roof has reached the end of its lifespan or sustained severe damage, it may be time for a roof replacement. Oraya Roofing specializes in expert roof replacement, using high-quality materials and superior craftsmanship to ensure a flawless installation that will withstand the elements for years to come.

Roof Installation

For new construction or roof replacements, our professional roof installation services ensure a reliable and durable roof for your property. We handle every aspect of the installation process with precision and expertise, using top-quality materials and employing skilled craftsmen. Trust Oraya Roofing for a roof installation that exceeds your expectations.

Solar Shingles

Go green and harness the power of the sun with our solar shingle installation services. Solar shingles integrate seamlessly into your roof, providing clean and renewable energy for your home or business. Our team of experts will design and install a solar roofing system that maximizes energy efficiency while enhancing the aesthetic appeal of your property.

Gutter Replacement & Installation in Miami FL

In addition to our roofing services, we also provide comprehensive gutter solutions. Properly functioning gutters are crucial for diverting water away from your property, preventing water damage, and maintaining the structural integrity of your roof. Our team can assist you with gutter installation, repair, and maintenance to ensure optimal performance.
